Hi! I’m a PhD candidate in Information Science at Cornell Tech, where I study practical methods for evaluating and improving socio-technical systems with Allison Koenecke and Rene Kizilcec. This summer, I'm working as a Responsible AI Research Intern for Apple. I am a member of the AI, Policy, and Practice (AIPP) initiative and a Digital Life Initiative (DLI) fellow. I have collaborated on research as part of Stanford's RegLab, Microsoft Research FATE, the Sloane Lab, the Compliant and Accountable Systems Group at the University of Cambridge, and the Algorithmic Justice League.

Previously, I worked as a data scientist at a consulting firm. I received my BA in Computer Science and Philosophy, Politics, & Economics from the University of Pennsylvania.

In my free time, you can find me reading vaguely upsetting novels or running very slowly. In fact, I'll be running the NYC Marathon this year, and am currently fundraising to support climate sustainability. If you would like to support this cause (and me!) by making a donation, I would so appreciate it.
